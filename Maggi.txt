To make an mouth watering maggi follow these step:
1 step:
Take an container add water and start boiling water
2 step:
Remove your maggi packet break the pieces of maggi and add into the container which consist your water
3 step:
Take some vegetables if you want (to make an healthy maggi)Chop them 
4 step:
Take maggi masala .
5 step:
Assemble all things and pour it into your container
6 step:
Exception:If you want to make it an spicy maggi add spices
7 step:
Add salt as per your taste
8 step:
Let your maggi boil 
Take a deep breath here your maggi is ready in 2 minute
Enjoy it and make you taste bud love it
